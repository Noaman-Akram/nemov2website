Why work here

The kind of place worth working at.

We're not a factory. We don't grind through briefs. We choose projects carefully and do them exceptionally well.

01
Small team, real impact

There are no layers here. What you make ships. Clients see your work. You own the outcome.

02
Craft over volume

We'd rather do four great projects than ten mediocre ones. You'll have time to think, not just execute.

03
Async-first, trust by default

No standups. No check-ins. You manage your time. We care about the work, not the hours.

04
Work that goes in your portfolio

Everything we ship looks good. That's not a coincidence — it's the point.

05
Honest, direct, no politics

Feedback is specific and fast. There are no hidden agendas. You know where you stand.

Life at Nemo

What it actually looks like.

No perks theatre. No foosball tables. Just good work, good people, and the freedom to do your best thinking.

Deep work by default

Meetings are rare and short. Most of your day is uninterrupted time to make things. That's the deal.

Picked clients, real briefs

We don't take every project that comes in. The work you're doing is for founders and companies we actually believe in.

Built to last

Everything gets documented. Design systems, not one-offs. You're building infrastructure, not decorations.

Fully remote

Work from anywhere. Cairo, Berlin, wherever. Results are what counts.

Competitive pay

Market rate or above. We don't lowball people we want to keep.

Equipment budget

Laptop, monitor, peripherals. Get what you need to work well.

Learning budget

Courses, books, conferences. We invest in your growth because it comes back.

Our process

No black boxes. Here's how we hire.

We move fast and respect your time. Most processes take 2–3 weeks from first call to offer.

01
Application

Apply with your portfolio and a short note about why Nemo specifically. No cover letter required.

1–2 days to hear back
02
Intro call

30 minutes with the founder. No prep needed — just a conversation about your work and how you think.

30 min
03
Work sample

A short paid exercise relevant to what you'd actually be doing here. We don't do spec work for free.

Paid · 3–4 hrs
04
Final call + offer

Meet the team, ask anything. If it's a fit, we move quickly. No deliberation theatre.

1–2 days to decision
