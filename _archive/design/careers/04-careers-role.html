Senior Product
Designer

About the role

We're looking for a Senior Product Designer who can take ambiguous problems and turn them into sharp, considered interfaces. You'll work directly with our product and engineering teams to define new product experiences across our AI platform — from zero to polished.

This is a high-ownership role. You'll drive design end-to-end: from discovery and strategy through to pixel-perfect delivery. We move fast and we care deeply about craft.

What you'll do

  • Lead design on 1–2 major product workstreams at a time
  • Run discovery sessions with customers and translate insights into product decisions
  • Design flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity specs that engineers can build directly from
  • Define and evolve the Nemo product design language
  • Partner with PMs to shape roadmap and prioritization
  • Mentor junior designers across the team

What we're looking for

  • 4+ years of product design experience, ideally at a software company
  • A portfolio that shows strong thinking — not just execution
  • Proficiency in Figma, and comfort working closely with engineers
  • Comfort navigating ambiguity and defining your own brief
  • Excellent communication — you can articulate design decisions clearly

Nice to haves

  • Experience designing data-heavy or AI-adjacent products
  • Motion / prototyping chops (Framer, ProtoPie)
  • Cairo-based or willing to spend meaningful time there

What we offer

  • Competitive salary, reviewed annually
  • Equity — you're building this with us
  • Flexible working (remote-first culture)
  • Annual learning & equipment budget
  • Access to a team that genuinely cares about craft

We're hiring a Senior Frontend Engineer to help build the interfaces that power our AI platform. You'll work at the intersection of performance, craft, and complexity — turning technically demanding requirements into experiences that feel effortless.

About the role

We're building interfaces, brand moments, and product experiences that need to move well. You'll work across product UI animation, marketing motion, and occasional brand films — with significant creative ownership over each.

This is a rare role. You'll report directly to our Creative Director and work closely with both product and brand teams. You won't be executing other people's briefs — you'll be generating ideas and owning them end to end.

What you'll do

  • Design and produce motion for product UI, onboarding flows, and empty states

  • Develop a motion language that sits alongside our visual identity

  • 3+ years creating motion for digital products or agencies
  • Mastery of After Effects, Lottie/Bodymovin, and at least one prototyping tool

  • Portfolio with real shipped work, not just personal projects
